Our idea

We want to create a virtual library of students' closets. Instead of asking around if someone has a drill, you can search in our platform and rent one from your classmate. We believe the peer-to- peer rental platform will enable us to share our things more efficiently.

Sustainability Values

Yale students are environmentally conscious. They want to make responsible purchase decisions. Y Circular offers a sustainable solution, without asking students to abdicate the perks of convenience.
